Early Life

Amr Diab was born on October 11, 1961 in Port Said, Egypt. He began singing and playing the piano at a young age, and later studied music at the Cairo Academy of Arts.

Music Career

Amr Diab began his music career in the 1980s, and quickly became one of the most popular singers in the Arab world. He has released over 30 albums, and has sold over 50 million records worldwide.

Awards

Amr Diab has won numerous awards throughout his career, including seven World Music Awards, four African Music Awards, and six Arabian Music Awards.

Personal Life

Amr Diab is married and has four children. He is known for his philanthropic work, and has established a foundation to help underprivileged children in Egypt.
